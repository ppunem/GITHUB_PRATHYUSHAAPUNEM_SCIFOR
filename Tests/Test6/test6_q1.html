NUTRITIONAL VEGGIES

Carrots contain many nutrients, including beta-carotene and antioxidants, that may support your overall health as part of a nutrient-rich diet.

Some benefits of eating beets may include lower blood pressure and better athletic performance, among others. Eating beets raw may be more beneficial than boiling them.

Radish benefits: Even though not everyone likes radish, it comes with a host of health benefits - from protecting the heart to aiding in digestion.
